**Endpoint:** `/api/mortgage-payment-schedule`

**Description:**  
Generate a detailed mortgage payment schedule based on loan parameters.

## Request

- **Method:** POST
- **Body:**  
  - `loanAmount` (number): The principal loan amount.
  - `interestRate` (number): Annual interest rate (percentage).
  - `termYears` (integer): Loan term in years.
  - `startDate` (string, optional): Start date of the mortgage.

## Response

- **200 OK:** Amortization schedule with payment breakdown.
