## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Sign Up">
    Visit [RealDealCPA.ai](https://realdealcpa.ai) and click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Information">
    Fill out the registration form with:

    - **Email**: Your valid email address
    - **Password**: A secure password for your account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Registration">
    Click "Sign up" to create your account and start your investment journey.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Already have an account? Click "Sign in to existing account" to access your dashboard.
</Note>

### Step 2: Access Your Dashboard

Once logged in, you'll see your dashboard with:

- **Navigation Menu**: Access Analyze, General settings, and Security options
- **Team Settings**: Manage your account preferences
- **Available Credits**: Track your remaining analysis credits (e.g., 56 Credits)

#### How to Analyze a Property:

1. **Navigate to the Analyzer**
   - Click on "Analyzer" in the main navigation
   - You'll see the property search interface
2. **Enter Property Details**
   - Type the property address in the search field
   - Example: "123 Main St, Los Angeles, CA" or any US property address
   - The system accepts full addresses with city and state
3. **Initiate Analysis**
   - Click the "Analyze Property" button
   - The AI will process the property data in real-time

<Warning>
  Each property analysis uses one credit from your available balance. Make sure you have sufficient credits before starting an analysis.
</Warning>

### Understanding the Data

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Cap Rate Interpretation">
    - **Above 8%**: Generally considered good for rental properties
    - **4-8%**: Moderate returns, typical for stable markets
    - **Below 4%**: Lower returns, may indicate appreciation-focused investment
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Cash Flow Analysis">
    - **Positive Cash Flow**: Property generates monthly income after expenses
    - **Negative Cash Flow**: You'll need to contribute monthly to cover expenses
    - **Break-even**: Income exactly covers expenses
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Risk Assessment Factors">
    - **Market Volatility**: How stable property values are in the area
    - **Cash Flow Stability**: Reliability of rental income
    - **Cap Rate Competitiveness**: How your returns compare to market averages
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Property Not Found">
    - Verify the address is correct and complete
    - Try using different address formats
    - Ensure the property is in the United States
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Analysis Taking Too Long">
    - Check your internet connection
    - Refresh the page and try again
    - Contact support if the issue persists
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Insufficient Credits">
    - Check your available credits in the dashboard
    - Purchase additional credits through the Pricing page
    - Contact support for credit-related questions
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
